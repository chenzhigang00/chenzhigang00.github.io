About DeepGuard

We are a team of researchers and engineers dedicated to combating the malicious use of AI-generated audio.

Our Mission

With the rapid advancement of generative AI, voice cloning and audio deepfakes have become indistinguishable from reality. DeepGuard was founded to provide the tools, research, and awareness needed to protect individuals and organizations from audio fraud.

Our focus spans from developing robust detection algorithms to understanding the ethical implications of synthetic media.

MISSION

Research First

We believe in open, rigorous scientific research as the foundation for effective defense strategies.

AI for Good

Developing AI technologies responsibly to solve security challenges rather than creating new threats.

Privacy Centric

Our detection methods are designed to respect user privacy and data security.
